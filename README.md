# Pigeon Creek Community Survey

A community survey collecting feedback on the ecological health, recreational access, and public awareness of Pigeon Creek in Evansville, Indiana.

Conducted by **UE Venturing Crew 365** and the **University of Evansville ChangeLab**.

**Take the survey:** [https://4eyefellow.github.io/pigeon-creek-survey/](https://4eyefellow.github.io/pigeon-creek-survey/)
